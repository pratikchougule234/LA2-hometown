Kumbhoj village in Hatkanangle Taluka

Kumbhoj (pronounced as kam'bho'j) is the name of an ancient town located in Kolhapur district in Maharashtra. The town is about eight kilometers from Hatkanangale, about twenty seven kilometers from Kolhapur and currently, also is the Taluka or Tehsil Headquarters[citation needed]. As per 2019 stats, Kumbhoj villages comes under Hatkanangle assembly & parliamentary constituency. Ichalkaranji is nearest town to Kumbhoj which is approximately 16km away.
